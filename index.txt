# about

About me

I'm a Data Analyst with experience since 2019 in Business Intelligence (BI), Data Warehousing (DWH), and Data Analytics consulting. I specialize in developing, implementing, and maintaining data warehouse extracts, transformations, and ETL processes across industries like Insurance, Hospitality, and Retail.

My goal is to turn complex data into actionable insights through intuitive Business Intelligence reporting in Power BI and Tableau. I am dedicated to making your data functional, accurate, and visually compelling, ensuring informed decision-making for your business.
